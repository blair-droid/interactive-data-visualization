## About the Dataset

The dataset contains behavioral information about approximately **4700 credit card customers** over a recent observation period.  
Each row represents a single customer and includes financial activity metrics such as purchases, balances, credit limits, and payment behavior.

The dataset is used to analyze relationships between spending behavior, credit usage, and repayment patterns.

---

## Variables

**CUST_ID**  
Unique identifier of the credit card holder.

**BALANCE**  
Remaining balance in the credit card account available for purchases.

**PURCHASES**  
Total amount of purchases made using the credit card.

**ONEOFF_PURCHASES**  
Amount spent on one-time purchases (single transactions).

**INSTALLMENTS_PURCHASES**  
Amount spent on purchases made in installments.

**CASH_ADVANCE**  
Cash withdrawn using the credit card.

**CASH_ADVANCE_TRX**  
Number of cash advance transactions.

**PURCHASES_TRX**  
Total number of purchase transactions.

**CREDIT_LIMIT**  
Maximum credit limit assigned to the cardholder.

**PAYMENTS**  
Total payments made by the cardholder.

**MINIMUM_PAYMENTS**  
Minimum required payments made by the cardholder.

**PRC_FULL_PAYMENT**  
Percentage of months where the customer paid the full balance.

---

## Frequency Variables

These variables measure how frequently certain actions occur. Values range between **0 and 1**, where:

- **1** = action occurs frequently  
- **0** = action occurs rarely or not at all  

**BALANCE_FREQUENCY** – frequency of balance updates  
**PURCHASES_FREQUENCY** – frequency of purchase activity  
**ONEOFF_PURCHASES_FREQUENCY** – frequency of single large purchases  
**PURCHASES_INSTALLMENTS_FREQUENCY** – frequency of installment purchases  
**CASH_ADVANCE_FREQUENCY** – frequency of cash advances

---

## TENURE

**TENURE**  
Number of months the customer has held the credit card.
